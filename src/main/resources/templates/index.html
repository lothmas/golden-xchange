How It Works

Step By Step

  • Register

    Step 1
    • If You Dn't Have an Account Register First
    • Provide All Required Details, If you dn't provide a profile picture a default system logo is selected for you.
    • Bank Accounts cn't be changed once created. Except via a Request to a System Admin via Live Chat
    Register

  • Sign-in

    Step 2
    • Use Your Registered Credentials To Login
    • If you have any login issue use live chat to resolve the issue
    Login

  • Create Donation

    Step 3
    • After logging-in go to the create donation section
    • Follow the given steps starting by confirming your bank account details
    • Provide the amount you want to donate & the money you will receive is calculated
    • After creating a donation you are redirected to a list of Sponsors were you will have to pay Your Sponsors 1st before a main payment is selected for you. If a main payment is not confirmed within 4hrs it is automatically cancelled and a new one is assigned.
    • Please Note: you have to use the provided depositReference when making payments.

  • Confirmation / Approvals

    Step 4
    • When you make payments you have to confirm payment by clicking confirm & wait for the recipient to approve havinging received
    • Notifications of a donation status are sent both to the system and via sms
    • When you receive a notification of a payment, please login and approve having received the funds
    • Note if you dn't complete the full payment you chose to donate you wn't be able to be paidback after your donation has matured
